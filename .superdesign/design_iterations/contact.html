Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and policies

We use only sustainable materials including organic cotton, hemp, linen, bamboo fiber, TENCEL™, and recycled fibers. All materials are sourced from certified suppliers who meet our strict environmental and ethical standards.

Each garment comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend cold water washing, air drying, and using eco-friendly detergents. Proper care helps extend the life of your garments and maintains their sustainable properties.

We offer free returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn with tags attached. We also accept gently worn items through our take-back program for recycling into new products.

Yes, we ship to over 10 countries worldwide. All international orders are carbon-neutral through our partnership with verified offset programs. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

We're certified by GOTS, OEKO-TEX, and FSC, with full supply chain transparency. We publish annual sustainability reports and welcome third-party audits. Every claim we make is backed by verifiable data and certifications.
