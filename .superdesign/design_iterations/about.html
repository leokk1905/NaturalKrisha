Our Journey

2019 - The Beginning

Founded by environmental science graduate Sarah Chen, who was frustrated by the lack of truly sustainable fashion options that didn't compromise on style.

2020 - First Collection

Launched our debut collection of 12 essential pieces made from organic cotton and hemp, selling out within three months.

2021 - Going Global

Expanded international shipping and partnered with environmental organizations to plant our first 1,000 trees.

2022 - Innovation

Introduced recycled materials and launched our circular design program, allowing customers to return worn pieces for recycling.

2023 - Community Growth

Reached 50,000 customers and launched our sustainability education platform, sharing knowledge about conscious fashion choices.

2024 - Future Forward

Continuing to innovate with new sustainable materials and expanding our impact through partnerships with regenerative farming initiatives.
